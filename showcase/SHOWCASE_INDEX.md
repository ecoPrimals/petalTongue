### [07 - BingoCube Visualization](./local/07-bingocube-visualization/)
**Purpose**: Demonstrate cryptographic visualization with BingoCube  
**Duration**: 10 minutes  
**Status**: ✅ Complete (NEW!)

**What you'll explore**:
- **Two-Board Cross-Binding**: Cryptographic hash of Board A + Board B → Color Grid
- **Progressive Reveal**: Control x parameter (0.0-1.0) for incremental trust
- **Interactive Generation**: Generate unique BingoCubes from different seeds
- **Visual Patterns**: Human-recognizable patterns (not hex hashes!)
- **Use Cases**: Identity verification, trust stamps, content fingerprints, computation proofs

**Success**: Understand how petalTongue makes cryptography human-verifiable

**💡 KEY INSIGHT**: BingoCube demonstrates petalTongue's power to make abstract concepts (cryptographic commitments) accessible through beautiful, recognizable visual patterns!
